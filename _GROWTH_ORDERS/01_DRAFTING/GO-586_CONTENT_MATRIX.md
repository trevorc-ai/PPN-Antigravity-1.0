## Video Description (YouTube / Descript embed)

PPN Portal is the first structured clinical documentation platform built specifically for psychedelic therapy practitioners. This video shows the full arc of the platform, from patient onboarding to real-time session monitoring to compliance-ready reports.

Watch to see how PPN Portal helps practitioners document every session with confidence, compare outcomes against a growing peer network, and generate the clinical records that institutions, insurers, and regulatory bodies require.

**What is covered in this video:**
- 0:00 -- Your clinical workspace
- 0:35 -- Starting a patient session
- 0:55 -- Phase 1 preparation and baseline intake
- 1:15 -- Phase 2 Dosing Cockpit: real-time vitals and event logging
- 1:40 -- Interaction Checker: safety screening before every session
- 2:05 -- Clinical Intelligence: benchmarks and outcomes
- 2:30 -- Report suite: PDF exports for compliance and institutional review

Visit ppnportal.net to learn more.
