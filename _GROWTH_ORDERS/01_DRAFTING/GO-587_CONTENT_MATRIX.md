Real-Time Clinical Intelligence: The PPN Portal Dosing Cockpit

## Video Description (YouTube / Descript embed)

During a psychedelic therapy session, every minute matters. The PPN Portal Dosing Cockpit plots your patient's heart rate, blood pressure, and temperature in real time, with automatic alerts when vitals cross clinical thresholds, and a permanent record of every event from the moment the session begins.

This video shows how the Dosing Cockpit works, what it records, and why it is the most clinically important feature PPN Portal offers.

**What is covered:**
- 0:00 -- The clinical question every practitioner should be able to answer
- 0:22 -- What the Vitals Trend Chart shows and how it is built
- 0:42 -- How session events are pinned to the timeline
- 1:00 -- Automatic threshold alerts for HR and blood pressure
- 1:18 -- The Live Session Timeline: every clinical event, timestamped
- 1:35 -- Logging a session update in real time

Visit ppnportal.net to learn more.
