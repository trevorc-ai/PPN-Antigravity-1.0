## üí° **THE CLINICAL WORKFLOW TRANSFORMATION**

### **Current Workflow (What We Were Building):**
1. Practitioner designs protocol (offline, in their head)
2. Practitioner delivers treatment
3. Practitioner logs protocol in PPN (retroactive)
4. Practitioner sees benchmarks later (days/weeks)

**Problem:** Data is **retroactive**, not **actionable in real-time**

---

### **NEW Workflow (What We Should Build):**

#### **BEFORE Treatment:**
1. Practitioner opens Protocol Builder
2. Enters patient characteristics (age, weight, condition, medications)
3. **SYSTEM SHOWS LIVE:**
   - Similar patients' outcomes (% success rate)
   - Receptor targets for proposed protocol
   - Safety considerations based on concomitant meds
   - Network benchmarks for this patient profile

4. Practitioner adjusts protocol based on data
5. **Practitioner shows patient the data:**
   - "Here's why we're choosing this approach"
   - "Here's what similar patients experienced"
   - "Here's how this will affect your brain chemistry"

6. Practitioner saves "planned protocol"

#### **DURING Treatment:**
7. Practitioner delivers treatment
8. Logs any deviations or safety events

#### **AFTER Treatment:**
9. Practitioner logs actual outcomes
10. **SYSTEM COMPARES:**
    - Planned vs actual outcomes
    - Patient's response vs similar patients
    - Clinic's outcomes vs network benchmarks

11. **SYSTEM LEARNS:**
    - Updates predictive models
    - Refines receptor impact estimates
    - Improves future recommendations
