Pricing & Billing  
Q: Is there a free trial?  
A: Yes\! All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to features. No credit card required to start.

Q: Can I switch plans later?  
A: Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?  
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) and ACH transfers for annual plans.

Q: Do you offer discounts for annual billing?  
A: Yes\! Save 20% with annual billing. Solo: $948/year (vs. $1,188), Clinic: $4,788/year (vs. $5,988).

Q: What happens if I exceed my user or site limits?  
A: For Clinic plans, you can add users ($40/mo each) or sites ($100/mo each) at any time. For Enterprise needs, contact us for custom pricing.

Security & Compliance  
Q: Is PPN HIPAA-compliant?  
A: Yes. PPN is fully HIPAA-compliant with bank-level encryption (AES-256), SOC 2 Type II certification, and signed Business Associate Agreements (BAA) for all customers.

Q: Where is my data stored?  
A: All data is stored in secure, HIPAA-compliant data centers in the United States (AWS US-East). We never store PHI outside the US.

Q: Can I export my data?  
A: Yes. All plans include data export (CSV). Clinic and Enterprise plans include scheduled exports and custom formats.

Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?  
A: You retain full access to your data for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all data during this period. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted per HIPAA requirements.
