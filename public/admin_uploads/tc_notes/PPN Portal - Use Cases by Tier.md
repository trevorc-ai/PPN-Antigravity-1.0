Tier 0: Grey Market / Underground Practitioners  
Profile: Operating in legal grey areas (Oregon, Colorado decriminalized zones), cannot use traditional EMR systems due to legal risk.

Pain Points  
Legal vulnerability: Client lists could be seized  
No proof of experience: Can't get licensed without incriminating themselves  
Unsafe dosing: Strain potency varies 3x (causes 911 calls)  
Dangerous clients: No vetting system (undercover cops, violent individuals)  
No legal defense: If session goes wrong, "no notes" \= negligence

WO\_006: Legacy Transcript \- Career insurance (anonymized proof of experience)  
WO\_043: Text Brightness Fix \- Comfort for dilated pupils  
WO\_045: Session Ambiance \- Professional atmosphere

"Safety & Defense Tools" in exchange for anonymized data

Zero-knowledge architecture (we can't give what we don't have)

Build resume for future legal market  
Community protection (bad actor warnings)
