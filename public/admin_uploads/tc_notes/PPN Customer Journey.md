Executive Summary  
The PPN Research Portal serves psychedelic therapy practitioners through a sophisticated journey that transforms them from curious visitors into strategic research partners. This analysis maps seven distinct stages, identifies key touchpoints, friction points, and opportunities for optimization.

Key Finding: The portal excels at safety and data visualization but has critical gaps in onboarding, activation, and partnership development that limit network effects and long-term value realization.

Conclusion  
The PPN Research Portal has built an exceptional foundation with world-class safety features, beautiful data visualization, and a clear value proposition. However, critical gaps in onboarding, activation, and partnership development are limiting growth and network effects.

The Path Forward:

Fix the Funnel \- Add pricing, improve onboarding, reduce time to first value  
Build Retention Loops \- Email notifications, mobile app, templates  
Enable Power Users \- Advanced features, API, custom dashboards  
Formalize Partnerships \- Advisory board, co-marketing, research collaboration  
By addressing these gaps systematically, PPN can transform from a powerful tool into an indispensable platform that practitioners can't imagine working without.
