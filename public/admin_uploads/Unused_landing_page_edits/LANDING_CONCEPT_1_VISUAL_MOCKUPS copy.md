## üé¨ THE VISUAL JOURNEY

### **SCENE 1: THE VOID (Hero)**
**Emotional State:** Lost in chaos, practicing in isolation

**Visual:**
- Dark void with thousands of scattered particles (representing fragmented insights)
- Single glowing portal in center (the way forward)
- Headline: "STANDARDIZED OUTCOMES. BENCHMARKED SAFETY."
- Stats: "12k+ RECORDS | 840+ CLINICIANS | 98% UPTIME"

**User Feeling:** "I'm lost in the dark, searching for structure"

### **SCENE 2: FIRST DISCOVERY - SAFETY CONSTELLATION**
**Transformation:** From reactive to proactive (safety surveillance)

**Visual:**
- Particles organize into hexagonal constellation (structure emerging)
- Seven glowing nodes connected by light beams
- Center: "SAFETY SURVEILLANCE" with shield icon
- Surrounding nodes show transformation benefits (not features)

**User Feeling:** "I'm discovering structure. I can anticipate problems before they happen."

**Mockup:** See `concept_1_constellation_scene2` image above

### **SCENE 3: SECOND DISCOVERY - NETWORK CONSTELLATION**
**Transformation:** From isolated to connected (global community)

**Visual:**
- Particles form circular network of 14 glowing towers
- Each tower represents a global site (Baltimore, London, Zurich, etc.)
- Light beams connect all towers (data sharing)
- Center: "840+ CLINICIANS CONNECTED"

**User Feeling:** "I'm no longer alone. I'm part of something bigger."

### **SCENE 4: THIRD DISCOVERY - INTELLIGENCE HELIX**
**Transformation:** From guessing to knowing (data-driven decisions)

- Each helix rung shows a capability (not a feature)
- Orbital stats: "98% Uptime", "HIPAA Compliant", "End-to-End Encrypted"

**User Feeling:** "I have the knowledge I need to make confident decisions."
