Platform Capability Overview

Platform Visual Brief

Six views from the PPN Portal, annotated for clinic owners and their clinical staff. Every screen shown here is live infrastructure, available to Founding Partners now.

Not mockups. Not prototypes. This is the platform your facilitators will use starting Day 14.

Clinic Performance Radar
What Your Team Sees

Real-time benchmarks for your clinic: Performance Radar across six dimensions (Efficacy, Safety, Retention, Compliance, Revenue, Speed) plotted against the anonymized network average, with Quarterly Analysis and patient outcome clustering.

Why It Matters for Your Practice

You now have a single view that tells you how your outcomes compare to the field, where your facilitators excel, and where you may need to intervene. No other tool in this space offers this legally.

02 of 06
Drug Interaction Checker
What Your Team Sees

Pre-session contraindication screening of the selected compound against the patient's full medication list, with pharmacological pathway, severity classification, and protocol citations.

Time to Value

60-Second Check Facilitators run a complete interaction screen before the patient sits down. Every check is automatically timestamped and logged, creating defensible standard-of-care documentation with zero additional effort.

03 of 06
Patient Efficacy Trajectory
What Your Team Sees

PHQ-9 plotted from baseline through follow-up intervals for each anonymized protocol, charted against the community median and clinical remission threshold. No patient identity is attached.

Why It Matters for Your Practice

Your facilitators can see exactly how a patient is trending, compare to network outcomes, and make confident decisions about follow-up paths, all without opening a single additional tab or document.

04 of 06
Compliance Audit Ledger
What Your Team Sees

A complete, filterable ledger of every clinical event: timestamp, demographic class (no names), substance, activity, status, and a cryptographic hash verifying record integrity since creation. No record can be altered or deleted by any user.

Why It Matters for Your Practice

If a state licensing board or compliance auditor requests documentation, you export this in one click. Your records are already formatted to meet 21 CFR Part 11 and Oregon OHA OAR 333-333 standards.

05 of 06
Post-Session Documentation
What Your Team Sees

Structured post-session input: facilitators log vitals, semantic observations (from a validated structured menu), adverse events, and integration recommendations. All selections are stored as validated data, never free text.

Time to Value

2-Minute Sync Facilitators complete a full post-session record in under 120 seconds. No end-of-day charting, no retrospective data entry, no lost observations. Documentation happens while the session is still fresh.

06 of 06
Network Benchmarking
What Your Team Sees

Quarterly benchmarking across six performance dimensions plotted against the anonymized network average, with scored breakdowns and plain-language context per dimension to guide operational decisions.

Why It Matters for Your Practice

For the first time, your clinic can see exactly where it stands in the broader field, without patient privacy risk. This is the operational intelligence that justifies PPN to your board, your investors, and your staff.
