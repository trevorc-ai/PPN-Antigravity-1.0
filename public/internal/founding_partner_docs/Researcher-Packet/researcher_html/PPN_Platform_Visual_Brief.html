Platform Capability Overview

Platform Visual Brief

Six core views from the PPN Portal Clinical Intelligence Platform, annotated for institutional partners. Each view reflects live infrastructure, available to Founding Partners now.

Not mockups. Not prototypes. This is the platform.

01 of 06
Clinical Intelligence Dashboard
What You Are Seeing

Real-time cross-site benchmarks: Performance Radar (six composite dimensions vs. network average), Quarterly Analysis with scored breakdowns, and Patient Galaxy outcomes clustering by Treatment Resistance and PHQ-9 severity.

Why It Matters

Because PPN stores no PHI, cross-site benchmarking is legally permissible. No legacy EHR can offer this without significant liability exposure.

02 of 06
Drug Interaction Checker
What You Are Seeing

Pre-session contraindication screening of the selected compound against the patient's full medication list, with pharmacological pathway, severity classification, and regulatory citations. Every check is cryptographically logged.

Why It Matters

Every check is timestamped automatically. Override codes and stop decisions are captured in the immutable audit ledger, creating defensible standard-of-care documentation without any additional practitioner input.

03 of 06
Efficacy Trajectory
What You Are Seeing

PHQ-9 plotted from baseline through confirmed days-post-session intervals for an anonymized protocol, charted against the community median and clinical remission threshold.

Why It Matters

Structured real-world evidence at the individual level: clean, standardized, directly exportable to SPSS, R, or Python, with no data wrangling and no IRB exposure from identifiable records.

04 of 06
Immutable Audit Ledger
What You Are Seeing

A complete, filterable ledger of every clinical and security event: timestamp, patient demographic class (no names), substance, activity type, status, and a truncated cryptographic hash verifying record integrity since creation.

Why It Matters

Structured for 21 CFR Part 11 compliance. Every record is hash-verified, non-editable, and exportable for regulatory submission or IRB audit. No entry can be deleted by any user.

05 of 06
Data Export Manager
What You Are Seeing

Four export categories: Patient and Clinical Records (Treatment Bundle, Outcomes PDF, Audit Reports), Research Data (CSV, JSON for SPSS/R), Compliance (21 CFR Part 11 Audit Logs), and Forms and Templates.

Why It Matters

Partners run their own queries against their own cohort data and download directly. No data engineering queue, no reformatting, no wait. The research pipeline is already built.

06 of 06
Clinic Performance Radar
What You Are Seeing

Quarterly benchmarking across six dimensions (Efficacy, Safety, Retention, Compliance, Revenue, Speed) plotted against the anonymized network average, with scored breakdowns and natural-language context per dimension.

Why It Matters

A network director now has a single view to understand which sites are performing, where adverse events concentrate, and how outcomes compare to the broader field. This visibility has not previously existed in this space.
