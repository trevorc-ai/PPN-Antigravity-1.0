Why This Changes Your Practice

You Become a Better Therapist
Because PPN tracks structured data across every session, your facilitators can see exactly how each patient is trending in real time, compare to network outcomes, and make confident, evidence-based decisions about next steps without relying on memory or paper notes.
Your Patient Sees Their Healing
Patients in integration often feel uncertain about their progress. Handing them a structured, beautiful record of their trajectory transforms an abstract emotional experience into a concrete clinical story. They can see, in numbers, that the work is working.
You Justify Your Pricing
High-quality psychedelic therapy commands premium pricing. The Patient Integration Story is a tangible clinical deliverable that demonstrates the rigor and sophistication of your practice in a way that a patient can show their GP, their family, or their insurance provider.
You Build Your Evidence Base
Every report generated is backed by structured, anonymized data in the PPN registry. Over time, your clinic's aggregate outcomes become real-world evidence of your protocols' efficacy, accessible to you and, with consent, to the research community.
