1 - The Clinician Founding Partner Relationship

This Charter establishes your clinic as a member of PPN's inaugural clinician cohort. This is not a standard software subscription. Clinician Founding Partners are selected for their clinical commitment, their community influence, and their alignment with the platform's mission to make psychedelic therapy safer and more evidence-based for every practitioner in the field.

In exchange for early adoption and participation in the network, your clinic secures a permanently locked-in institutional rate, direct influence over the product roadmap, and access to the clinical intelligence network that no standard EHR can provide.

2 - PPN's Commitments (12-Month Term)

  • Dedicated Clinic Instance: A fully partitioned registry instance configured for your clinical workflows. Your patient data is logically isolated and never commingled with other clinics without explicit consent.
  • Phantom Shield (Zero-PHI Architecture): Clinical infrastructure to log every session, track longitudinal outcomes, and document protocols without ever capturing, storing, or transmitting a patient's identity. Full subpoena resistance by design.
  • Network Benchmarking Access: Real-time anonymized benchmarks from the PPN network. See how your clinic's efficacy, safety, and retention outcomes compare to the broader field, legally and automatically.
  • Patient Integration Reports: One-click generation of the Patient Integration Story, a clinical deliverable your facilitators hand to patients at their integration session showing their healing trajectory.
  • White-Glove Onboarding: PPN-led staff training, workflow alignment, and 24/7 dedicated support channel. Your team is operational within 14 days of signing. No workflow downtime. No IT burden.
  • Direct Roadmap Input: Quarterly access to the PPN product team. Clinician Founding Partner requests are documented and carry formal weight in roadmap prioritization.

4 - Data Sovereignty

Your clinic retains 100% ownership of your clinical records at all times. PPN acts solely as a secure, structured registry. No partner data is shared with third parties or used commercially without explicit written consent. Upon termination, all data is fully exportable and, upon request, purged from PPN infrastructure.

5 - Confidentiality & Term

Both parties agree to keep the terms of this Charter and commercial pricing confidential. This Charter is effective for 12 months from the Effective Date, renewing annually unless either party provides 60 days' written notice.
