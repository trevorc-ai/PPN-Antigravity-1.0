Compliance Assurance

How PPN Survives a State Audit on Your Behalf, Automatically

Psychedelic therapy operates in one of the most heavily scrutinized regulatory environments in healthcare. State licensing boards, DEA inquiries, and HIPAA enforcement actions are a fact of life for clinics in this space. What separates clinics that survive these audits from those that don't is rarely the quality of their care. It is the quality of their documentation infrastructure.

PPN was designed to make your clinic audit-ready from Day 1, without adding any administrative burden to your facilitators or clinical staff. Every compliance deliverable below is generated automatically by the platform as a byproduct of normal documentation.

Federal and State Compliance Coverage

Electronic Records & Signatures
21 CFR Part 11 Compliance
All clinical events are hash-verified, timestamped, and immutable at the moment of creation. No record can be altered or deleted by any user, including administrators. The full audit ledger is exportable as a signed CSV for regulatory submission. This is the same standard used in FDA-regulated clinical trials.
Protected Health Information
HIPAA: Not Applicable by Architecture
PPN captures zero PHI by design. No patient names, dates of birth, addresses, or identifying information are ever entered or stored. HIPAA enforcement requires the existence of PHI to trigger. By capturing none, your clinic is architecturally exempt from HIPAA breach liability on PPN data. This is not a legal workaround. It is the design.
Oregon Psilocybin Services Act
OHA OAR 333-333 Protocol Alignment
PPN's contraindication alert system references OHA OAR 333-333 and the MAPS Phase 3 Protocol Manual in all issued warnings and override audit records. If your facilitator proceeds past a contraindication, the override decision is automatically captured with a required reason code and clinical rationale, creating a defensible record of clinical judgment.
