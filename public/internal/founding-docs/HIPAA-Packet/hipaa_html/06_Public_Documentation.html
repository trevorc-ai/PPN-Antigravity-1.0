Privacy Policy Content Summary

The Privacy Policy (src/pages/PrivacyPolicy.tsx) makes the following material claims, all of which are supported by the technical evidence in this packet:

  • Patient ages are generalized into ranges, not stored as exact values (under remediation — see Section 5)
  • Patient codes are system-generated and cannot be reverse-engineered (confirmed — see Section 3)
  • No patient name, date of birth, or identifying information is stored (confirmed — see Section 3)
  • All database access is protected by Row Level Security (confirmed — live schema)
  • Vendors are limited to Supabase, Vercel, Stripe, and Resend (confirmed — see Section 3)
Action Required Before Sending to Counsel

The Privacy Policy contains a claim that ages are stored as ranges, but the current schema stores them as exact integers. This inconsistency should be noted in the cover transmittal to counsel and resolved before filing. The age bucketing remediation (see Section 5, item D22a) resolves this issue. Once deployed, the Privacy Policy will be factually accurate on all points.
