Safe Harbor Table

HIPAA Privacy Rule Safe Harbor requires removal or generalization of 18 specific identifier categories (45 CFR §164.514(b)(2)). This table documents PPN Portal's status against each identifier as of March 2026.

All 18 Safe Harbor Identifiers

#Identifier CategoryPPN StatusEvidence / Notes
1NamesNever collectedNo patient name field in any database table. Confirmed via live schema review.
2Geographic subdivisions smaller than a stateNever collectedNo zip code, county, city, or address field in any patient-facing table.
3Dates (except year) — including birth date, admission date, discharge date, date of deathCompliant by designAge is stored as a foreign-key reference to a controlled vocabulary of 10 generalized brackets (ref_age_ranges), not as an exact integer or date of birth. Brackets group ages ≥ 90 together per HHS Safe Harbor guidance. No exact date or time data is stored in patient-linked tables. Session ordering uses ordinal sequence numbers, not calendar dates.
4Phone numbersNever collectedNo patient phone field. Practitioner phone not collected.
5Fax numbersNever collectedNot applicable to the platform.
6Email addressesNever collected (patient)Practitioner email collected for account purposes only. No patient email field.
7Social security numbersNever collectedNo SSN field anywhere in schema.
8Medical record numbersNever collectedNo MRN field. Patient codes are system-generated, not derived from MRNs.
9Health plan beneficiary numbersNever collectedNo insurance or health plan data collected.
10Account numbersNever collected (patient)Practitioner subscription IDs (Stripe) are not patient data.
11Certificate or license numbersNever collectedNo practitioner license number collected.
12Vehicle identifiers and serial numbers, including license platesNever collectedNot applicable to the platform.
13Device identifiers and serial numbersNever collectedWearable device data is logged as readings only, not device serial numbers. No device ID in schema.
14Web URLs (patient-identifying)Never collectedNo patient URL stored.
15IP addressesNot retained in patient recordsVercel and Supabase may retain request logs per their standard policies, but no IP address is stored in any PPN patient or clinical record.
16Biometric identifiers, including fingerprints and voiceprintsNever collectedNo biometric data collected.
17Full-face photographs and any comparable imagesNever collectedNo image upload capability for patient records.
18Any other unique identifying number, characteristic, or code — unless otherwise permittedCompliantPatient codes are system-generated random values using a 32-char unambiguous alphabet. Not derived from any patient attribute. Cannot be reverse-engineered. See Section 3 for pseudonymization spec.

Summary: 16 of 18 Safe Harbor identifiers are satisfied by the platform's zero-collection design. Identifier #3 (dates) and #18 (unique codes) are addressed by design — age stored as generalized ranges and patient codes generated as non-reversible random values — and should be reviewed by counsel for final confirmation.
