Part 1: Interoperability Standards

PPN enforces standardized clinical vocabularies at the point of data entry, ensuring all exports are immediately compatible with SPSS, R, Python, and SAS, conforming to global clinical trial standards. Full technical specifications — including LOINC code mappings, UCUM unit definitions, and ontology version references — are provided in the companion Technical Specifications & Interoperability Standards document.
