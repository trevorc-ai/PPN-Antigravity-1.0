1 - The Founding Partner Relationship

This Charter establishes the Founding Partner as an exclusive member of the PPN Clinical Intelligence Platform's inaugural partner cohort. This is not a standard commercial subscription. Participants are selected for their methodological rigor, institutional credibility, and alignment with the platform's research architecture.

In exchange for early adoption, structured data contribution, and program feedback, the Founding Partner secures a permanently locked-in institutional rate, direct influence over the product roadmap, and access to network intelligence unavailable through any other channel.

2 - PPN's Commitments (12-Month Term)

  • Dedicated Research Instance: A fully partitioned, white-labeled registry instance configured for your cohort. Data is logically isolated and never commingled without explicit written consent.
  • Phantom Shield (Zero-PHI Infrastructure): Clinical infrastructure to log sessions and track longitudinal outcomes without capturing, storing, or transmitting PHI. All records issued cryptographic synthetic Subject IDs at point of entry.
  • Network Benchmarking Access: Real-time access to anonymized, aggregated safety and efficacy benchmarks from the broader PPN network in a legally compliant, de-identified format.
  • Structured Data Export: Access to the PPN Session Export Center for longitudinal dataset exports in CSV/JSON, conforming to the Clinical Data Taxonomy & Export Dictionary.
  • White-Glove Onboarding: Dedicated support to align the platform with your clinical workflows, IRB requirements, and practitioner training needs.
  • Direct Roadmap Input: Structured quarterly access to the PPN product team. Founding Partner requests carry documented weight in roadmap triage.

4 - Data Sovereignty

The Founding Partner retains 100% ownership of their clinical records at all times. PPN acts solely as a secure, structured registry for anonymized benchmarking. No Partner data is shared with third parties or used commercially without explicit written consent. Upon termination, all data is fully exportable and, upon request, purged from PPN infrastructure.

5 - Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep the terms of this Charter, commercial pricing, and the existence of this program confidential. Neither party will reference the other in public communications without prior written consent.

6 - Term & Termination

Effective for 12 months from the Effective Date, renewing annually unless either party provides 60 days' written notice. Either party may terminate for cause with 30 days' written notice.
