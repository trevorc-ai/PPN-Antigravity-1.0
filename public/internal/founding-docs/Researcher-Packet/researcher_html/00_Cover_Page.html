Psychedelic-Assisted Therapy Infrastructure  ·  Institutional Research Briefing

The Infrastructure Gap
in Psychedelic Research

A Real-World Evidence Registry purpose-built for longitudinal PAT studies — Zero PHI, full interoperability, and immediate IRB alignment for multi-site psychedelic clinical research.

The Problem
Fragmented EMR silos and PHI regulations make cross-site psychedelic outcome data legally inaccessible for meta-analysis.
The Architecture
Zero-PHI by design. Cryptographic Subject IDs eliminate breach liability and subpoena risk at the host institution level.
The Outcome
Longitudinal PAT telemetry immediately compatible with SPSS, R, and Python. No data wrangling. No IRB exposure from identifiable records.
