Part 3: Audit & Compliance Architecture

Electronic Records
21 CFR Part 11
All clinical events are hash-verified, timestamped, and immutable. No record can be altered or deleted by any user, including administrators. Full ledger exports as CSV.
Protected Health Information
HIPAA
Not applicable — PPN captures zero PHI by architectural design. No names, dates of birth, or identifying information are ever entered or stored.
Oregon Psilocybin Services Act
OHA OAR 333-333
Contraindication alert system references OHA OAR 333-333 and the MAPS Phase 3 Protocol Manual in all issued warnings and override audit records.
