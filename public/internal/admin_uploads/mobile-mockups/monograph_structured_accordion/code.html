Dosage

insights Receptor Affinity Profile

Primary agonist at 5-HT1A and 5-HT2A receptors. Exhibits high binding affinity for the 5-HT1A receptor subtype, which may contribute to its distinct psychological effects compared to DMT.

Glutamatergic modulation in PFC
Sigma-1 receptor chaperone activation
warning Critical Alert

Strictly avoid combination with MAOIs (Monoamine Oxidase Inhibitors) due to risk of serotonin syndrome. Potential for cardiac stress in predisposed individuals.

CAS Registry 1019-45-0

Formula C13H18N2O
