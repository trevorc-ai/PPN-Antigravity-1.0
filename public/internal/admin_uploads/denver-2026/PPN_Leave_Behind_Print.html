The Practitioner Network

Every session logged
makes you smarter.

Because our database structure never changes, PPN can generate real-time visual analytics that let every practitioner benchmark their outcomes against the growing global peer network — making every practitioner smarter with every session logged. When you have documented 30 preparation sessions, the system knows you are a preparation specialist. When a colleague needs that expertise, the network surfaces you. Opt-in. Pseudonymous. Governed.
Clinic Performance Radar
Quarterly benchmarking across Efficacy, Safety, Retention, Compliance, and Speed — plotted against the anonymized network average. See exactly where your clinic stands without patient privacy risk.
Cross-Site Benchmarking
Because PPN stores no PHI, it is the only platform that can legally benchmark outcomes across an entire clinical network — a capability no standard EHR can offer.
Efficacy Trajectory
PHQ-9 and GAD-7 scores plotted from baseline through follow-up intervals for each anonymized protocol. Chart patient progress against the community median and clinical remission thresholds.

Research-ready CSV exports for IRB, investor, or compliance review — no data wrangling required. Export a 21 CFR Part 11 compliant audit ledger with one click.
