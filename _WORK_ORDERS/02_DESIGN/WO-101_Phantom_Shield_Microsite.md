**"How do I know my data is safe?"**
- **The Screenshot:** Show a mock "Database View" of a log table.
- **The Detail:**
  - Column 1: `Session_ID` (UUID)
  - Column 2: `Patient_ID` -> `****************` (Masked)
  - Column 3: `Outcome_Score` -> `8.2` (Visible)
  - *Caption:* "What our servers see vs. What you see. Your client names never leave your device."
- **Why:** Showing a "boring" table with masked fields creates more trust than 1000 words of copy about "AES-256 encryption".
