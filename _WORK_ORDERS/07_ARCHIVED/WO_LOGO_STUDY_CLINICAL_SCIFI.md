# Minimalistic "Clinical Sci-Fi" Logo Study & Visual Identity

## 1. THE GOAL

Develop a minimalistic visual identity and logo for the PPN Research Portal that aligns with the "Augmented Intelligence" brand positioning.

### Logo Analysis
Conduct a study of current high-end medical-tech and AI data visualization logos.

### Minimalistic Design
Propose 3-5 logo concepts based on the following parameters:
* **Style:** Ultra-minimalist, high-fidelity, "Clinical Sci-Fi."
* **Motifs:** Abstract representations of neural networks, molecular structures, or data "hives."
* **Typography:** Pair the mark with a clean, monospace or high-legibility sans-serif typeface.

### Color Palette Integration
The logo must be designed to pop against the Deep Slate (#020408) background using Aurora gradient highlights (Clinical Green, Cyan, or Electric Purple).

### Scalability
Ensure the mark remains legible at favicon sizes (16x16px) and high-resolution header displays.

### Accessibility
The logo must maintain high contrast ratios and not rely on color alone for recognition (ensure the silhouette is distinct).
