### **Pain Point #1: Software/Workflow Failure** üî¥ CRITICAL
**The Problem:**
> "Administrative burnout from fragmented tools is killing us"

**Current Reality:**
- Practitioners patch together 4-5 disconnected tools
- IntakeQ + Spruce + Excel + SimplePractice + Osmind
- Context switching = 5-10 hours/week lost
- **No single "operating system" for psychedelic practice**

**Strategic Opportunity:**
- **TAM Expansion:** 3x larger (includes ALL practitioners, not just data-oriented ones)
- **Pricing Power:** $999/month ("operating system") vs $399/month ("data tool")
- **Switching Costs:** Once integrated, hard to leave
- **Network Effects:** Multiplied (more touchpoints = more lock-in)

**Missing Use Cases:**
1. **Client Intake** - Replace IntakeQ
2. **Client Messaging** - Replace Spruce
3. **Scheduling** - Replace SimplePractice calendar
4. **Document Storage** - Replace Google Drive chaos
5. **Billing Integration** - Link to existing billing systems

### **Pain Point #2: Supervision & Mentorship Vacuum** üî¥ CRITICAL
**The Problem:**
> "I feel isolated. The unique challenges of PAT require specialized supervision that's hard to find"

**Current Reality:**
- No centralized marketplace for peer supervision
- Expensive consultations ($ 200-400/hour)
- Post-certification "cliff" - practitioners abandoned
- **Underground Telegram/Signal groups** = current solution

**Strategic Opportunity:**
- **New Revenue Stream:** Matchmaking fees (20% of supervision transactions)
- **Stickiness:** Community = retention
- **Viral Loop:** Practitioners invite colleagues for supervision circles
- **Competitive Moat:** Network effects (more supervisors = more value)

**Missing Use Cases:**
1. **Supervisor Directory** - Searchable, verified credentials
2. **Dyad Matching** - AI-powered compatibility matching
3. **Supervision Circle Formation** - Group supervision (4-6 practitioners)
4. **Case Consultation Forums** - Private, verified-only discussions
5. **Mentorship Program** - Senior practitioners mentor juniors
