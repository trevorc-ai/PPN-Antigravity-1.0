# Frictionless Word-of-Mouth & Mobile Sharing Tools

**Objective:** Equip founders, early adopters, and network participants with zero-friction, highly visual assets they can text, airdrop, or present via QR code to spread the PPN product.

## 1. The "Phantom Shield" Share Card (Already Live)

We have already built a dedicated, mobile-optimized "Share Card" that acts as a digital business card for the platform's primary value proposition (Zero-PHI safety).

*   **Location:** `public/phantom-shield-card.html` (Accessible live at `ppnportal.net/phantom-shield-card.html`)
*   **What it is:** A beautifully designed, glowing "iOS Wallet" style card that is designed entirely for a mobile screen. 
*   **How it works:** 
    *   It features a large, scannable QR Code right in the center.
    *   It has a native iOS/Android "Forward" button. If the user clicks it, it opens their phone's native share sheet (Messages, AirDrop, WhatsApp) with a pre-written hook: *"Protect your practice. Your data never exists."*
*   **The Use Case:** When Jason (or any practitioner) is at a conference or dinner and someone asks about liability, they don't send a long URL. They just pull up this card on their phone, hand it across the table, and let the other person scan the QR code to instantly be taken to the Phantom Shield overview.

## 2. The "Network Architect" Invite Link 

The most powerful word-of-mouth driver in B2B SaaS is professional vanity and network building. We should map out a strategy for personalized invite links.

*   **The Concept:** Instead of an anonymous "Sign up here" link, practitioners generate invites that say, *"Dr. Smith has invited you to join their Clinical Intelligence Network on PPN."*
*   **The Mechanism:** Every user gets a dedicated `/invite/[their-name]` vanity URL.
*   **The Incentive:** It frames the inviter as an early-adopter "Architect" of the clinical data network, bringing their peers onto the system. 

## 3. The "Clinical Efficacy" Export Card (Future Roadmap)

Practitioners are proud of their outcomes. We need to give them a way to share those outcomes safely on LinkedIn, Twitter, or in WhatsApp groups.

*   **The Concept:** A 1-click "Share to Social" button on the Analytics dashboard.
*   **How it works:** It generates a high-resolution, branded PNG image (an infographic) of their clinic's aggregated outcomes (e.g., *"Our clinic achieved an 82% Responder Rate using the PPN benchmarked protocols."*). It strips out all specific protocol data and patient info, acting purely as a marketing asset for their clinic that happens to feature the "Powered by PPN" logo.
*   **The Use Case:** Practitioners use PPN to generate marketing material for their own social media, accidentally driving massive word-of-mouth visibility for the PPN software wrapper.
