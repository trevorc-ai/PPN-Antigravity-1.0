## Executive Summary

This revised logo study presents **5 cosmic, portal-inspired logo concepts** for the PPN Research Portal, drawing inspiration from the "Portal Sequence" landing page concept. Each design features a **circular/ring-based form** with **soft, inviting glows** and **subtle incorporation of double helix and yin-yang motifs**. These concepts are **artistic, nuanced, and professional** - designed to feel safe, welcoming, and transformative rather than sharp or overly geometric.

**Design Philosophy:** *Cosmic transformation through subtle symbolism*

**Recommended Concept:** **Concept 2 - "Eclipse Helix"** (glowing ring with subtle helix spiral)

## Usage Guidelines

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the radius of the ring on all sides.

### Glow Intensity
- **Light backgrounds:** Reduce glow opacity by 50%
- **Dark backgrounds:** Full glow effect
- **Print:** Remove glow, use solid ring only

### Animation Potential
All concepts can be subtly animated:
- **Concept 1 (Cosmic Ring):** Gentle pulsing glow (breathing effect)
- **Concept 2 (Eclipse Helix):** Helix spiral slowly rotates (10s cycle)
- **Concept 3 (Yin-Yang Eclipse):** Luminosity shifts subtly (yin-yang balance)
- **Concept 4 (Orbital Helix):** Nodes orbit around ring (15s cycle)
- **Concept 5 (Nebula Portal):** Particles drift slowly (ambient motion)

### Prohibited Uses
- âŒ Do not add harsh drop shadows (use soft glows only)
- âŒ Do not make ring perfectly circular (keep subtle organic warp)

## Implementation Recommendations

### File Formats Required
1. **SVG** - Primary format with glow effects (scalable)
2. **PNG** - With transparency and glow (16px, 32px, 64px, 128px, 256px)
3. **ICO** - Favicon format (16px, 32px, 48px multi-resolution)
4. **PDF** - Print-ready vector (no glow, solid ring)

### SVG Implementation Example
